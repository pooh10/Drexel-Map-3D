About Our Project

Drexel University's campus can be a confusing place to navigate, even for those who have been here for years.

There are dozens of academic buildings, food places, recreational centers, and more.

The Drexel University 3D Map project aimed to create detailed 3D models of all Drexel University buildings, offering an innovative way to explore our campus while showcasing its architectural diversity.

We've created detailed 3D models of all Drexel buildings, providing an immersive experience for students, faculty, and visitors.

Our Team Charter

Definition of Done

  • The task in question is functional above all else, and usable by other members.

  • 3D-models need to be distinctively accurate to their real life counterparts.

  • A website for documentation & to showcase our models and let users see the 3D models.

Working Agreement

  • We communicate (with Discord or iMessage)

  • We contribute equal amounts of effort

  • We are coordinated in our efforts.

  • We finish the project on time

Communication & Protocols

  • A Discord server will be created to help with organizing everything.

  • Responses need to be made be in a timely manner (15-30 minutes).

  • If someone is actually required, it's best call them.
